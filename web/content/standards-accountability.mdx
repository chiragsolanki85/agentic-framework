## Decision ownership by stage

- Foundations -> Product Lead
- Requirements -> Product Lead
- Technical Specification -> Engineering Lead
- Implementation/PR -> Engineering Lead
- Context Complete -> Engineering Lead + Product Lead

## Core principle

Humans always own final decisions, risk acceptance, and approvals regardless of agent autonomy.
