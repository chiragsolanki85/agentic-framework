## Core

Required baseline for production-safe delivery and governance.

## Extended

Recommended controls for stronger auditability and risk management.

## Optional

Context-dependent artefacts that remain hidden unless needed.

## Adoption strategy

Start with Core, add Extended by risk tier, and activate Optional only by explicit decision.
