## Purpose

Foundations establish immutable project context across product, architecture, engineering, data, and ways-of-working. No feature delivery should begin until foundations are complete and approved. Weak foundations increase rework and risk downstream.

[Explore this stage in the SDLC Flow](/flow#foundations)

## Inputs

Strategic intent, business constraints, team structure, and existing technical landscape.

## Outputs

Complete foundation package across five domains plus cross-cutting controls and architect handoff.

## Prompt to run

`framework/prompts/01-foundations-facilitator.md` — creates all foundation files including the readiness checklist.

## Human gate

**Owner:** Product + Engineering + Delivery  
**Decision:** Approve foundation readiness before requirements work begins.
