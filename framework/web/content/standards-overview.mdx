## Why standards exist

Standards are the guardrail layer that keeps agentic delivery fast but controlled.

They define:

- how work is executed
- who approves what
- what evidence must exist before progression
- how risk is contained

## Standards set in this framework

- Coding Standards
- Security and NFR Standards
- Artefact Governance Rules
- Traceability Policy
- Accountability Matrix
- V2 Lifecycle Governance

## Enterprise expectation

No stage progression should rely on implicit understanding. Standards are explicit, versioned, and auditable.
