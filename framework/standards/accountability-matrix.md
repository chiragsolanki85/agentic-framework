# Accountability Matrix

## Stage approvals

| Stage | Required artefact(s) | Decision owner (approver) | Contributors |
|---|---|---|---|
| Foundations | `context/00_foundations/**/*.md` | Product Lead | Engineering, Delivery, Design |
| Requirements Definition | `context/01_requirements/features/*` + refined gate | Product Lead | Engineering, Design |
| Technical Specification | `context/02_specs/SPEC-*` | Engineering Lead | Product, Senior Engineers |
| Implementation/PR | `context/03_implementation/*` + PR evidence | Engineering Lead | Engineers, Reviewer Agent |
| Context Complete | `context/logs/context/*` + updated shared context | Engineering Lead + Product Lead | Team |

## Human accountability rule

Humans own final decisions, risk acceptance, and approvals at every gate regardless of agent autonomy.
